## Demo Tips

### Do's
- ✅ Prepare test data beforehand
- ✅ Practice the flow multiple times
- ✅ Have backup plans for technical issues
- ✅ Highlight key features clearly
- ✅ Show real-time updates
- ✅ Answer questions confidently
- ✅ Keep it concise (10-15 minutes)

### Don'ts
- ❌ Don't rush through features
- ❌ Don't show bugs or errors
- ❌ Don't skip important features
- ❌ Don't use real customer data
- ❌ Don't make promises you can't keep
- ❌ Don't overcomplicate the demo

## Handling Questions

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How much does it cost?**
> "We offer flexible pricing plans based on your needs. Basic plans start at $99/month for small restaurants, with enterprise plans available for larger operations. We can discuss pricing based on your specific requirements."

**Q: Do you integrate with POS systems?**
> "Yes, we support webhook integrations with major POS systems and ordering platforms. We can also build custom integrations for your specific system."

**Q: What about mobile apps?**
> "Currently, we have a fully responsive web app that works great on mobile devices. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are planned for Phase 2."

**Q: How do you handle payments?**
> "Currently, restaurants handle payments directly. We're planning to integrate payment processing in Phase 2, allowing customers to pay through the platform."

**Q: Can we use our own riders?**
> "Absolutely! TekMax is designed to work with your own delivery staff first. Courier partnerships are available as a backup option when your riders are unavailable."

**Q: How does real-time tracking work?**
> "Riders update their location via the mobile web app or future native app. The system uses GPS to track their location and updates all stakeholders in real-time via WebSocket connections."

**Q: What if a delivery fails?**
> "The system handles failed deliveries with proper status tracking. Restaurants can reassign deliveries, and we track failure reasons for analytics and improvement."

**Q: How do you ensure data security?**
> "We use industry-standard security practices: encrypted data transmission, secure password hashing, JWT authentication, and regular security audits. We're also GDPR compliant."
